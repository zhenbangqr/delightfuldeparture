Her last bit of dignity is taken care of by the thoughtful "angel in white". Considering the dignity of the female deceased, Nirvana Group is the first in Malaysia to be exclusively female. From embalming, cleansing, makeup and burial, men are never allowed to contact, touch or even watch, and the female deceased are given the highest respect.


Preserving the dignity of your departed loved ones.The White Ladies are a team of professionally trained female embalmers and beauticians dedicated to rendering their immaculate services with a genuine heart for deceased female body.
