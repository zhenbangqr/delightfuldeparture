About Us

Delightful Departure,
Founded in 1977 by LIOW ZHEN BANG

Established in 1977 by the visionary Liow Zhen Bang, Delightful Departure has emerged as a beacon of compassion and innovation in the realm of funeral services. This remarkable journey began with the profound aspiration to transform the way society approaches the delicate matter of bidding farewell to our dearly departed loved ones.

Liow Zhen Bang, more than just a founder, is a compassionate and dedicated individual who recognized the need for a more profound and personalized approach to funerals. His journey was driven by a deep understanding that each person's life is unique, and their farewell should reflect the spirit and individuality that defined them.

At Delightful Departure, we have redefined the very essence of funeral services. We take pride in offering a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients, ensuring that every aspect of their farewell is handled with utmost care and compassion.

Delightful Departure,
Not just only a simple funeral company.

Our services extend far beyond the conventional norms of the industry. We specialize in crafting custom coffins that not only serve as a final resting place but also as a symbol of the unique life they encapsulate. Additionally, our state-of-the-art columbarium provides a serene and dignified resting place for those who choose cremation.

Understanding the logistical challenges during such emotional times, we offer transportation services for coffins and families, ensuring that the journey is as smooth and comforting as possible. We also provide essential goods for those in need, making the process more convenient for grieving families.

Delightful Departure is proud to offer a diverse range of religious services, including Buddhist and Christian ceremonies, ensuring that each farewell aligns with the spiritual beliefs and traditions of the departed and their family.

Our White Angel Service is a testament to our commitment to creating an atmosphere of serenity and solace during these challenging times. Our Dancing Pallbearers, a unique and heartfelt service, adds a touch of celebration to the proceedings, reminding us that even in sorrow, there can be moments of joy and remembrance.

For those who seek spiritual comfort and protection, we offer the services of an exorcist, ensuring that the farewell is conducted with utmost reverence and positivity.

In summary, Delightful Departure, founded by the compassionate Liow Zhen Bang, is not merely a funeral service company; it is a symbol of empathy, innovation, and personalized care. Our journey is driven by a deep commitment to transforming farewells into memorable tributes that truly embrace the spirit and uniqueness of each individual's life.
